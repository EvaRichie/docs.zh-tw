<span data-ttu-id="c2bde-104">由於 'class' 具有 ComImport 屬性，'method' 必須為 extern 或 abstract</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c2bde-104">Since 'class' has the ComImport attribute, 'method' must be extern or abstract</span></span>
