<span data-ttu-id="2f124-182">IoT 指的是將網路連線在一起的實體物件。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f124-182">IoT refers to physical objects that are networked together.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2f124-183">它們有時稱為「連線裝置」或「智慧型裝置」。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f124-183">They're sometimes referred to as "connected devices" or "smart devices."</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2f124-184">車輛和自動販賣電腦的所有專案都可以連線，並將資訊從清查到感應器資料（例如溫度和濕度）傳送。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f124-184">Everything from cars and vending machines may be connected and send information ranging from inventory to sensor data such as temperature and humidity.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2f124-185">在企業中，IoT 透過監視和自動化來供應商務程式改進。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f124-185">In the enterprise, IoT provides business process improvements through monitoring and automation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2f124-186">IoT 資料可用來管制大型倉儲中的氣候，或透過供應鏈來追蹤清查。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f124-186">IoT data may be used to regulate the climate in a large warehouse or track inventory through the supply chain.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2f124-187">IoT 可以感知化學，並在偵測到冒煙時呼叫消防部門。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f124-187">IoT can sense chemical spills and call the fire department when smoke is detected.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="2f124-188">大量的裝置和資訊通常會指定事件驅動架構來路由傳送和處理訊息。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f124-188">The sheer volume of devices and information often dictates an event-driven architecture to route and process messages.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2f124-189">無伺服器是很理想的解決方案，原因如下：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f124-189">Serverless is an ideal solution for several reasons:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="2f124-190">可在裝置數量和資料增加時進行調整。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f124-190">Enables scale as the volume of devices and data increases.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="2f124-191">加入新的端點以支援新的裝置和感應器。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f124-191">Accommodates adding new endpoints to support new devices and sensors.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="2f124-192">促進獨立的版本控制，讓開發人員可以更新特定裝置的商務邏輯，而不需部署整個系統。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f124-192">Facilitates independent versioning so developers can update the business logic for a specific device without having to deploy the entire system.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="2f124-193">復原和較少的停機時間。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f124-193">Resiliency and less downtime.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="2f124-194">IoT 的普及率導致數個無伺服器產品，特別著重于 IoT 考慮，例如 [Azure IoT 中樞](/azure/iot-hub)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f124-194">The pervasiveness of IoT has resulted in several serverless products that focus specifically on IoT concerns, such as [Azure IoT Hub](/azure/iot-hub).</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2f124-195">無伺服器自動化工作，例如裝置註冊、原則強制執行、追蹤，甚至是將程式碼部署到 *邊緣的*裝置。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f124-195">Serverless automates tasks such as device registration, policy enforcement, tracking, and even deployment of code to devices at *the edge*.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2f124-196">邊緣指的是連線到網際網路（但不是使用中的）的裝置（例如感應器和傳動器）。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2f124-196">The edge refers to devices like sensors and actuators that are connected to, but not an active part of, the Internet.</span></span>
